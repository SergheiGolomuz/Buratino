Russian Language School "Buratino"

We work together with the government programme Community Languages Services, which promotes and develops the teaching of community languages used by children of school age from the minority groups.

We offer Russian Language classes to children from 4 years and older. The aim of our classes is to nurture the interest in Russian language and culture in our pupils and to encourage the use of Russian language as a language of communication in bilingual children.

Our teaching methods include the following:

  • articulatory and phonemic exercises
  • development games;
  • arts and crafts;
  • reading and writing;
  • learning about the world around us and forming the understanding of the surrounding environment from the bilingual experience

Payment for the lessons is £10.00 per session.


Lessons take place on Sundays from 10:00-12:00 at: Cantell School,
Violet Road, Bassett, Southampton, Hampshire, SO16 3GJ, United Kingdom

* We also prepare pupils for GCSE and A-level Russian exams
