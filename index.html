Russian Speaking Community is a volunteer led organisation. Our goal is to enable interested people to meet, communicate, learn and explore the Russian language and the culture of societies where that is used in a welcoming, safe and inclusive atmosphere.

Russian Language School «Buratino» in Southampton has been in existence for the twelfth year. In many ways, this unique project was made possible thanks to the support of the city administration, as the city has a long-standing successful language program, the purpose of which is to help communities preserve and cultivate their mother tongue in children.

Thanks to this support, we were able to provide Russian-speaking families with the opportunity to teach their children their native language. The school is developing dynamically, and now about forty students come to us. This suggests that our project is very necessary and in demand, since for many children living in Southampton this is the only opportunity not only to learn Russian language and literature, but also to get in touch with the culture, traditions of the Russian people, and even just to communicate in their native language with peers.
